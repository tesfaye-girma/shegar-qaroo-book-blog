The impact of technological advancement on our daily lives/


In today's rapidly evolving world, information is highly valuable and communication is an essential. To meet this demand, we have different mediums such as cellular phones, television broadcasting and radio broadcasting. Although these medium help in mass communication, they arent as effective as internet. Internet at its most basic is a gigantic network of computers.

Addunyaa ammayyaa tana keessatti haala saffisaan jijjiiramaa jiru kanaan, odeeffannoo barbaachisaa cimaa ta’ee fi waliigaltee (kominikeeshinii) ammoo murteessaa dha. Gaaffii kana deebisuuf, meeshaalee garaagaraa qabna — fakkeenyaaf bilbila harkaa, televizhinii f i raadiyoo. Haa ta’u malee, meeshaaleen kun waliigaltee bal’aa keessatti tumsanii iyyuu, intarneetii akka isaan caalaa bu’a qabeessa miti. Intarneetii, salphatti yoo ilaalle, walitti dhufeenya koppiitara hedduu gidduutti uumame (network guddaa) dha.

    The concept of internet began in 1960s as a US-army-funded research project. It then evolved into a public infrastructure in the 1980s which was accessible but limited to universities and private companies. Gradually, people started to pick up on it and today internet is the fastest and most used medium of communication. A network is a link between two or more users which allows them to communicate whether it be a mobile phone or computer. As this network grows in size, it is labelled LAN, MAN and WAN. As internet grew in popularity, its uses became more advanced and reliable.

    Today, Internet is used around the entire globe for purposes ranging from entertainment to business. General population surf the internet for information which could be latest news, an instruction manual or on a search for a job. Similarly, private companies and businesses advertise themselves on the internet to reach a large audience. E-commerce, business through the internet, has become quite common in every business as more and more users are connected to the internet for longer periods of time. The usage of internet is limitless and is bound by the human imagination.

    A fire provides warmth and light when used appropriately but can burn if handled improperly. Internet has a similar gist to this. Since the information is not censored, incorrect information can quickly spread. False information can be difficult to identify and thus general audience can be misinformed. Similary due to lack of content censorship, pornography can be accessed by any user. Social media platforms are also misused by underage children as well as people people waste way too much time on these platforms. There are many issues that make internet a tether to the people but it depends on the person and the purpose of the use.

    Thus, internet is a communication medium that has a major role in todays modern world where work without internet is not imaginable. Internet is fast to deliver information but it is unmoderated. Censorship is implemented in some countries to restrict access but they are easily bypassed. Hence, internet should be used intelligently to make a positive impact.
